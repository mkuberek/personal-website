# project overview

## about the site

This is the personal website for **Matteo Kuberek** - a self-taught day trader who shares his trading knowledge and expertise through social media.

## purpose

The site serves as a digital business card, providing:
- Brief introduction to Matteo and his work
- Links to social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok)
- Direct contact information
- Professional online presence

## target audience

- Aspiring day traders
- Trading community members
- Potential collaborators
- People interested in learning about day trading

## design philosophy

**Minimalist & Direct**
- Single-page application
- Clean, elegant design
- No unnecessary elements
- Focus on content and accessibility
- Professional yet approachable

**No Portrait**
- Abstract or trading-themed visuals
- Focus on content rather than personal imagery
- Modern graphics and imagery

## technical details

### stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 13+ (App Router)
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment**: Vercel (automated via GitHub)
- **Package Manager**: Yarn

### architecture
- Single-page site (no backend)
- Component-based structure
- Responsive design
- SEO optimized
- Fast loading times
